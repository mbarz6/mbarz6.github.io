Reddit, YouTube, etc. are designed to give you frustration, anger, and stress.

What do you get out of them? Some distraction, and some quasi-socialization.

Breath in for 5 seconds, breath out for 3 seconds, and then ask yourself what you really need. Can you go back to work?

If you need distraction, please either listen to music (if you need noise while studying) or pick up a book (if you need a leisurely distraction).

If you need socialization, just put a Discord message down or send someone a text.
