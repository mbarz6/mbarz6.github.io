Math note: Max Mudpuppy's submarine modeled the ocean as a hyperbolic plane. This is why such a strange integral has such nice curves of minimal `length.' More generally, you can replace the normal arclength form ds2 = dx2 + dy2 with formulas like ds2 = dx2 + dy2/y^2 (hyperbolic) or more generally ds2 = a(x, y) dx2 + b(x, y) dxdy + c(x, y) dy2.
