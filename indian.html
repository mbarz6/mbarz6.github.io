Diving Dolphin Deals

This dolphin programmer is hiding underwater.

Recently, a group of Indian Ocean humpback dolphin scientists sold a copy of their computer algebra system, Riding Sine Waves, to Max Mudpuppy. Max Mudpuppie's favorite number is the cube root of 2. Unfortunately, Riding Sine Waves is only capable of doing arithmetic with rational numbers and matrices of rational numbers. This greatly angered Max Mudpuppy, who is now threatening violence against the providers of Riding Sine Waves unless they either refund his purchase or allow him to compute with the cube root of 2. 

Dolphin etiquette forbids one from offerring a refund to a terrorist, and so the only choice that remains is to add cube root of 2 arithmetic into Riding Sine Waves. 

Riding Sine Waves already has built-in matrix arithmetic. One of the dolphins on the design team, Kat, recalls that there is a 3 by 3 matrix M with integer coefficients obeying the equation M3 = 2I, where I is the 3 by 3 identity matrix. If they can find that matrix M, they can perform cube root of 2 arithmetic without needing to re-design their software!

Find a 3 by 3 matrix M which has integer coefficients and cubes to 2I. Input the coefficients of that matrix.
